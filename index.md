Building AI agents that operate in Europe? This series gives you practical compliance guidance for the EU AI Act, with real code examples using [Microsoft's open-source Agent Governance Toolkit](https://github.com/microsoft/agent-governance-toolkit), honest gap analysis, and no vendor spin.

**Why now?** August 2, 2026 is when high-risk AI system obligations and Article 50 transparency requirements become enforceable. Compliant governance is an ongoing legal requirement, not a one-time checklist.

Written by [Carlos Hernandez](https://www.linkedin.com/in/carloshvp), founder of [GenAI Gurus](https://genai-gurus.com) — Europe's GenAI practitioner community. For a curated index of EU AI Act tools, official sources, and templates, see [Awesome EU AI Act](https://genai-gurus.com/eu-ai-act).
